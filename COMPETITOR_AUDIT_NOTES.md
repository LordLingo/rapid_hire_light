# §83 — Top-5 BGC Competitor Audit (working notes)

## Top 5 (consensus across IBISWorld, Gartner, GoodHire, multiple 2025/2026 lists)

1. **First Advantage** — IBISWorld names it the #1 US background check business by share; Sterling acquisition closed 2024 making it the dominant enterprise CRA.
2. **Sterling** (now part of First Advantage) — historically #2; still operates as its own brand and site.
3. **Checkr** — #1 in tech/gig vertical; modern API-first; high G2/Gartner ratings.
4. **HireRight** — global enterprise, very large; Gartner-listed.
5. **GoodHire** (a Checkr company) — SMB-focused; consistently #1 on SMB lists.

(Honorable mention: Accurate Background — already used as inspiration in §81 — and Cisive.)

Will audit each competitor's site for: services structure, free tools/calculators, integrations, compliance/resources content, pricing transparency, conversion UX, candidate experience, social proof.
